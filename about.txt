Joadia
--------

Code implements a complete version of the "Disaster on Joadia Islands Wargame"

Original wargame concept and rules developed by Peter Williams (Joint and Operations Analysis Division, DSTG)

Joadia code written by Martin Wong, Dmitri Kamenetsky, Slava Shekh, Glennn Moy (Joint and Operations Analysis Division, DSTG) and by Tait Reid & Heean Park (Elmtek)
